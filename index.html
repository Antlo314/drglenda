The Centerpiece

Welcome to the UMOF Entrepreneurship & Business Development Programs

A Community Economic Empowerment Program dedicated to educating, equipping, and empowering aspiring and existing entrepreneurs through workforce development, financial literacy, business education, and funding readiness training — promoting self-sufficiency, wealth creation, and community development.

Transform ideas into successful businesses

We help individuals turn ideas into successful businesses by providing practical education, real-world business strategies, and access to resources that support long-term entrepreneurial success. Entrepreneurship is a powerful pathway to economic independence, generational wealth, job creation, and stronger communities.

Whether you are launching your first business, seeking capital to grow, improving your financial literacy, or preparing to pursue grants, contracts, loans, or investor funding — our programs deliver the knowledge, tools, and confidence to achieve your goals.

  • Build financially sound and sustainable businesses
  • Increase business knowledge and financial literacy
  • Strengthen business credit and funding readiness
  • Access capital and growth opportunities
  • Create jobs and economic impact in your community
  • Develop leadership and business management skills
  • Build wealth through business ownership

About the Organization

A nonprofit empowering minds & communities

Unlimited Mind of Freedom, Inc. (UMOF) is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization founded in 2003 and officially incorporated in 2019 by Dr. Glenda S. WilliamsCFWF. Headquartered in Lithonia, Georgia, UMOF is dedicated to empowering individuals and strengthening communities through education, entrepreneurship, and access to essential resources.

Through innovative programs, outreach initiatives, and community partnerships, UMOF addresses social, hunger, educational, and economic challenges — inspiring individuals to achieve long-term stability and success.
